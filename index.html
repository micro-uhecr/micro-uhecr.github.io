About

MICRO is a DFG funded, international effort with the main goal to understand the origins of Ultra-High Energy Cosmic Rays.
By improving the simulation tools, as well as combining experimental data and simulations, we expect to improve our understanding of the distributions of UHECR sources and how much they emit of other messengers like gamma rays and neutrinos.
This project involves multiple scientists from several universities and research centers in France and Germany.
